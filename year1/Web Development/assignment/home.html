Welcome!

Coffee Café is a locally owned café, situated in Ballsbridge. We specialise in serving high-quality coffee sourced exclusively from Fairtrade and Rainforest Alliance certified suppliers. A summary of our menu is available here.

We are located at the heart of Ballsbridge within easy access of other facilities in the area. For contact information, please refer to the Contact Us page.

In addition to our spacious main area, we have the capacity to host gatherings of large groups with our well-equipped conference room. If you wish to inquire about booking our conference room, please send an inquiry using the Bookings page to check availability and prices.

We are open 7 days a week, between the hours of 8AM and 7PM on weekdays and 10AM and 9PM at weekends.
